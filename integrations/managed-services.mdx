---
title: Managed services
description: Integrations that require coordination with your PolyAI account manager.
---

These integrations are not available as self-service in the Studio UI. They require setup by your PolyAI account manager.

1. Contact your PolyAI account manager or representative
2. Provide the required credentials and configuration details (see individual pages)
3. PolyAI configures the integration in your sandbox environment for testing
4. After successful testing, deploy to production

<Note>Some integrations may require additional agreements or licensing with the third-party provider.</Note>
