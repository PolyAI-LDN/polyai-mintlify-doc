## Overview

Design My Night is a restaurant and venue booking platform that helps hospitality businesses manage reservations and customer experiences. The PolyAI integration enables your AI agent to interact with the Design My Night system for booking management.

## Capabilities

- **Availability checking**: Query real-time table availability
- **Reservation creation**: Create new bookings with customer details
- **Booking management**: Retrieve, modify, and cancel existing reservations
- **Venue information**: Access venue details, opening hours, and capacity
- **Customer data**: Manage customer profiles and booking history

## Configuration

To integrate PolyAI with Design My Night, you'll need to configure the following parameters:

### Required credentials

- **API key**: Your Design My Night API authentication key
- **Venue ID**: Identifier for your venue in the Design My Night system
- **API endpoint**: Design My Night API base URL

### Optional parameters

- **Booking policies**: Cancellation policies, deposit requirements, and booking rules
- **Special requirements**: Handling of dietary requirements, accessibility needs, and special occasions

### Integration setup

Contact your PolyAI representative to configure the Design My Night integration with your specific credentials and venue settings.

## Usage

Once configured, your PolyAI agent can:

1. Check availability for specific dates, times, and party sizes
2. Create new reservations
3. Retrieve booking details
4. Modify existing bookings
5. Process cancellations
6. Access customer preferences and history

## Support

For assistance with the Design My Night integration, contact your PolyAI support team or your Design My Night account manager.
