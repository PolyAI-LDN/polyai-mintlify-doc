## Introduction

PolyAI supports integration with Microsoft Dynamics 365, enabling virtual agents to access CRM data and log interaction outcomes. This integration is managed through the PolyAI platform alongside other supported CRM integrations like Salesforce, Zendesk, and Gladly.

## Capabilities

- **Version tracking** — deployment records are tracked per Dynamics 365 integration, supporting environment and version management
- **CRM data access** — agents can retrieve customer records and context during conversations
- **Interaction logging** — conversation outcomes and metadata can be synced back to Dynamics 365

## How to set up

1. Contact your **PolyAI account manager** to enable the Dynamics 365 integration for your project.
2. Provide the required credentials and configuration details for your Dynamics 365 instance.
3. PolyAI will configure the integration and confirm when it is ready for use.
