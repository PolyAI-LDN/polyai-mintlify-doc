Connect PolyAI to telephony, CRM, booking, payment, and data platforms. Integrations let your agent access external systems during calls — look up reservations, create support tickets, process payments, or hand off to live agents through your contact center.

## Choosing an integration

Most integrations fall into one of these categories:

| Category | Purpose | When to use |
|----------|---------|------------|
| **Voice & telephony** | Route calls through your existing contact center | You already use a platform like Genesys, Five9, or Amazon Connect and want PolyAI to handle calls within that infrastructure. |
| **Reservations** | Look up, create, or modify bookings | Your agent needs to manage restaurant or hotel reservations during calls. |
| **CRM & support** | Read/write customer records and tickets | Your agent needs caller context or should create/update tickets during interactions. |
| **Healthcare** | Access patient-facing systems | You're deploying in healthcare and need EHR integration. |
| **Payments** | Process payments securely mid-call | Your agent needs to collect payment information with PCI compliance. |
| **Data & utilities** | Connect to data sources and services | Your agent needs address lookup, translation, or spreadsheet access. |

<Note>
Some integrations can be connected directly from the **Integrations** page in Agent Studio with click-based setup. Others require coordination with your PolyAI account manager. See individual integration pages for setup details.
</Note>

## Getting access

To enable an integration for your project:

1. Contact your PolyAI account manager or reach out to [PolyAI Support](mailto:support@poly.ai)
2. Provide the required credentials and configuration details (see individual integration pages)
3. PolyAI will configure the integration in your sandbox environment for testing
4. After successful testing, deploy to production

<Note>Some integrations may require additional agreements or licensing with the third-party provider.</Note>
