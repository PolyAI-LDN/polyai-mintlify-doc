## Overview

LiveRes by Zonal is a comprehensive restaurant booking and table management system. The PolyAI integration enables your AI agent to check availability, create reservations, modify bookings, and manage customer information directly within the LiveRes platform.

## Capabilities

- **Availability checking**: Query real-time table availability across venues and time slots
- **Reservation creation**: Book tables with customer details, party size, and special requirements
- **Booking modifications**: Update existing reservations including time, party size, and customer preferences
- **Booking cancellation**: Cancel reservations through the AI agent
- **Customer management**: Access and update customer profiles and preferences
- **Multi-venue support**: Manage bookings across multiple restaurant locations

## Configuration

To integrate PolyAI with LiveRes, you'll need to configure the following parameters:

### Required credentials

- **API key**: Your LiveRes API authentication key
- **Venue ID(s)**: Identifier(s) for your restaurant venue(s)
- **API endpoint**: LiveRes API base URL (typically provided by Zonal)

### Optional parameters

- **Default booking duration**: Standard reservation length (e.g., 90 minutes)
- **Booking lead time**: Minimum advance notice required for reservations
- **Maximum party size**: Largest party size that can be booked online

### Integration setup

Contact your PolyAI representative to configure the LiveRes integration with your specific credentials and business rules.

## Usage

Once configured, your PolyAI agent can:

1. Check table availability for specific dates, times, and party sizes
2. Create new reservations with customer information
3. Retrieve existing booking details
4. Modify or cancel reservations
5. Access customer history and preferences
