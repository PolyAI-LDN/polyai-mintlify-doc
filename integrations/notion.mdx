The Notion integration enables your agent to search for pages and retrieve page content from a connected Notion workspace.

## Connecting Notion

### Prerequisites

- A Notion workspace with appropriate permissions
- Project management permissions in PolyAI

### Connection steps

1. Go to the **Integrations** page in Agent Studio
2. Find **Notion** and click **Connect**
3. Select an environment (Sandbox, Pre-release, or Live)
4. Click **Connect new account**
5. Authenticate with your Notion workspace via OAuth
6. You'll be redirected back to PolyAI automatically

## Available actions

| Action | Category | Description |
|--------|----------|-------------|
| **Search Pages** | Knowledge Base | Search for pages across a connected Notion workspace |
| **Get Page By ID** | Knowledge Base | Retrieve the content of a specific Notion page by its ID |

## Enabling actions

Reference Notion actions in your agent's prompts — either enable all actions or select specific ones. See the [Zoom integration](/integrations/zoom#enabling-zoom-actions) for detailed examples of this pattern.

### Example use case

**Internal knowledge base lookup:**
```
You are a support agent. When you need to look up internal documentation,
search the Notion workspace for the relevant page and use its content
to answer the customer's question.
```

## Troubleshooting

| Issue | Solution |
|-------|----------|
| Integration not appearing | Verify the feature is enabled for your account and you have project management permissions |
| Agent can't find pages | Ensure the Notion pages are shared with the connected integration |
| OAuth errors | Ensure pop-ups are not blocked, clear browser cache, or try a different browser |
