Custom dashboards are built for your specific business needs. Unlike the [standard](/analytics/dashboards/standard) and [safety](/analytics/dashboards/safety) dashboards which are available to all customers, custom dashboards are designed in collaboration with PolyAI to track the metrics that matter most to your organization.

![dashboard-1](/images/analytics/custom-dashboard-1.png)

## What custom dashboards include

Custom dashboards can track any combination of metrics relevant to your use case. Common examples include:

| Metric | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Containment rate** | Percentage of queries resolved by the agent without escalation or [handoff](/call-handoff/introduction) |
| **Booking performance** | Success rates, conversion funnels, and failure reasons for booking interactions |
| **Call resolution breakdown** | Categorized call outcomes (resolved, transferred, abandoned) with drill-down capability |
| **Call flows and outcomes** | Visual maps of customer journeys showing which paths lead to successful resolution |
| **Revenue impact** | Bookings completed, upsells, or payments processed by the agent |
| **Custom function metrics** | Performance data from your specific [functions](/tools/introduction) and integrations |

## Dashboard platform

Custom dashboards are powered by **Amazon Quick** (formerly Amazon QuickSight) and are embedded directly in Agent Studio. You access them through the Studio interface – no separate login or external tool is required.

## Available metrics in the chart editor

When you build or edit a chart widget, the metric picker lists every metric the platform can plot. This includes your own [custom metrics](/settings/metrics) written via `conv.write_metric`, plus the following platform-computed metrics that are populated automatically:

| Metric | Source | What it tracks |
|--------|--------|----------------|
| `POLY_SCORE` | [PolyScore](/analytics/polyscore) | Overall 0–10 conversation quality score |
| `POLY_SCORE_NORMALIZED` | [PolyScore](/analytics/polyscore) | Normalized PolyScore for cross-period comparison |
| `POLY_SCORE_AGENT_QUALITY` | [PolyScore](/analytics/polyscore) | Agent Quality sub-score (conversation flow and customer experience) |
| `POLY_SCORE_TASK_SUCCESS` | [PolyScore](/analytics/polyscore) | Task Success sub-score (whether the user's request was resolved) |
| `MATCHED_TOPIC` | [Managed topics](/managed-topics/introduction) | A managed topic was matched in the conversation |
| `CITED_TOPIC` | [Managed topics](/managed-topics/introduction) | A managed topic was cited in the agent's response |

These metrics appear in the picker even if you have not configured any custom metrics, so you can chart PolyScore trends or topic coverage out of the box. They are scoped to voice conversations where the underlying feature is available – see the linked pages for eligibility details.

## How to get a custom dashboard

Custom dashboards are available to enterprise customers. Contact your PolyAI account manager to:

1. Define the KPIs and metrics you want to track.
2. PolyAI designs and builds the dashboard based on your requirements.
3. Review and iterate on the design in your Sandbox environment.
4. Deploy the dashboard for your team.
