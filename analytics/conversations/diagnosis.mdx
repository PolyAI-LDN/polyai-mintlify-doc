---
title: "Diagnosis"
description: "Understand the function and topic calls your agent made during the call."
---

The **Conversation Diagnosis** menu on the Conversation Review page provides deeper insights into the agent's behavior. Users can toggle views to examine:
- **Function calls**: Details the API or system actions invoked during the call.

![conversations-function](/images/conversations-function-call.png)

- **Flows**: Tracks the agent's navigation through the call structure.
- **Topic citations**: Highlights the knowledge base topics the agent relied on to generate responses.

![conversations-topics](/images/conversations-matched-topics.png)
