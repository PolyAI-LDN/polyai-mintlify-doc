A **view** is a saved combination of filters and column settings on the [Conversations](/analytics/conversations/review) table. Views turn one-off filtering into a repeatable workflow you can pin, share with your team, and set as your default.

Views are kept separate per channel – Voice and Webchat each have their own pinned tabs.

## Concepts

- **View** – a saved combination of filters and column settings.
- **System View** – a built-in view (Production or Test) that everyone gets by default and that can't be edited or deleted.
- **Custom View** – a view you create and pin as a tab.
- **Filters** – conditions that narrow the conversations table (PolyScore, duration, environment, custom metrics, and so on).
- **Column configuration** – which columns are visible, in what order.

<Info>
You can save up to **50 Custom Views per project**, per scope. Custom View names are limited to **25 characters**.
</Info>

## System Views

Every project starts with two System Views per scope. They cannot be renamed, edited, or deleted:

| System View | Filter | Use it for |
|---|---|---|
| **Production** | Live environment | Monitoring real customer conversations |
| **Test** | Sandbox, draft, and pre-release environments | Working in Sandbox, debugging branches, QA before deploying to Live |

If you spend most of your time in Test, save your filters as a Custom View and **set it as default** so the page opens there.

## Create a Custom View

<Steps>
  <Step title="Apply your filters">
    Open **Analytics > Conversations**, pick a scope (Voice or Webchat), and apply the filters and column choices you want.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save changes">
    When the current view has unsaved changes, a **Save changes** button appears in the views toolbar. Click it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Name the view">
    Enter a name (up to 25 characters – a counter shows how many you have left) and confirm. The view appears as a new pinned tab with a pin icon.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Manage views

Open the **three-dot menu** on any pinned tab to access view actions:

| Action | Shortcut | What it does |
|---|---|---|
| **Rename** | – | Update the view name in place. |
| **Set as default** | – | Open this view automatically when you visit the page. |
| **Duplicate** | <kbd>Ctrl</kbd>+<kbd>D</kbd> | Create a new Custom View with the same filters and columns. |
| **Share filters as link** | <kbd>Ctrl</kbd>+<kbd>Shift</kbd>+<kbd>L</kbd> | Generate a shareable URL encoding the current filter state. |
| **Delete** | – | Remove the Custom View. System Views can't be deleted. |

Switching between views is a single click – no page reload needed.

## Share a view with your team

Use **Share filters as link** to copy a URL encoding your current filter and column setup. When a teammate opens the link:

- A banner appears at the top of the page letting them know that a shared filter set is applied **temporarily**.
- They can choose **Dismiss** to return to their previous view, or **Save as new view** to add it to their own pinned tabs.

This way, sharing a link never silently overwrites someone's existing setup.

## Set a default view

Open the three-dot menu on the tab you want as your default and choose **Set as default**. The next time you open the Conversations page, that view loads automatically. Only one Custom View per scope can be the default at a time.

## Limits

- View names are capped at **25 characters**.
- **50 Custom Views maximum** per project, per scope.
- System Views (Production, Test) cannot be renamed, edited, or deleted.
- Views are scoped per channel – a Voice view doesn't appear on the Webchat scope and vice versa.
