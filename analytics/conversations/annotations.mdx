---
title: "Annotations"
descriptions: "Mark issues for PolyAI quality control."
---

From the Conversation Review page, users can annotate responses to identify areas for improvement:
- **Wrong transcription**: Marks ASR (automatic speech recognition) issues.
- **Missing topic**: Flags gaps in the assistant's knowledge base.

![conversations-error](/images/conversations-wrong-transcription.png)

Annotations can be:
1. Shared with the PolyAI team for updates and refinements.
2. Used for self-service updates to enhance the knowledge base.
