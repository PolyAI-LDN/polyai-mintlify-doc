Voice settings control how your agent sounds when speaking to callers. You configure voice from **Channels > Voice** in the sidebar.

For voice interactions, the agent converts its text responses into speech using text-to-speech (TTS) providers like ElevenLabs, Cartesia, AWS Polly, and others. Voice settings let you choose *which* voice to use and *how* it sounds — including stability, clarity, and speed.
