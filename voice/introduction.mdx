Voice is a fundamental aspect of human communication, and it plays a pivotal role in shaping the perception of your brand. ## [Voice Configuration](./how-to-configure-voice)

In the **Voice Configuration** tab, you can tailor the voice of your assistant to match your preferences and requirements. Choose from a selection of voices and adjust parameters such as stability, clarity, and similarity to create a voice that aligns perfectly with your brand identity.

Selecting the right voice for your assistant is crucial in creating an authentic and engaging experience for your customers. Whether you're aiming for professionalism, friendliness, or sophistication, PolyAI's technology enables you to find the perfect match for your business image or use case.
