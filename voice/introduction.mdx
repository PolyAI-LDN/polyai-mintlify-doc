Configure your agent's voice in **Channels > Voice** in the sidebar.

## Voice configuration

Select a voice and adjust parameters like stability, clarity, and similarity to match your brand.

See the [Agent Voice configuration page](./agent) for step-by-step instructions.

<Tip>Configure voices programmatically using the [voice class](/function/classes/voice).</Tip>

## Choosing a voice

Match the voice to your brand tone—professional, friendly, or conversational. Test different options to find the best fit.

## [Custom voice request](./custom-voice-request)

Enterprise clients can request a custom voice using PolyAI's voice cloning technology.

## [Multi-voice](./multi-voice)

Assign multiple voices to a single agent to simulate a team of agents. Configure this in any function.
