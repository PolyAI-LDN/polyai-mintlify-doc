The **Voice library** provides a centralized place to explore, preview, and select voices for your agent. Access it from the **Agent Voice** page under **Channels > Voice**.

<Note>
The Voice library replaces the previous voice selection interface with an enhanced browsing experience including filters, favorites, and custom text preview.
</Note>

## Accessing the Voice library

1. Go to **Channels > Voice > Agent Voice** in the sidebar.
2. Click **Change** next to either the **Assistant** or **Disclaimer** voice section.
3. The Voice library opens with two tabs: **Explore** and **Favorites**.

## Explore tab

Browse all available voices with powerful filtering options.

### Filtering voices

Use the filter controls to narrow down your search:

| Filter | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Language** | Filter by supported language (e.g., English, Spanish, French) |
| **Region** | Filter by accent or regional variant (e.g., US, UK, Australian) |
| **Gender** | Filter by voice gender |

### Voice cards

Each voice displays:

- **Voice name** and provider
- **Language** and region flags
- **Style tags** describing the voice characteristics
- **Play button** to preview the voice

### Previewing voices

1. Click the **play button** on any voice card to hear a sample.
2. Enter **custom text** in the preview field to hear the voice speak your own content.
3. Compare multiple voices before making a selection.

<Tip>
Preview voices with text that matches your agent's typical responses to ensure the voice fits your use case.
</Tip>

## Favorites tab

Save voices you frequently use or want to evaluate later.

- Click the **heart icon** on any voice card to add it to Favorites.
- Access your saved voices quickly from the **Favorites** tab.
- Remove voices from Favorites by clicking the heart icon again.

## Selecting a voice

1. Find a voice using **Explore** or **Favorites**.
2. Click the voice card to select it.
3. Click **Select** to apply the voice to your agent.

The selected voice is applied to either the **Assistant** or **Disclaimer** section depending on which **Change** button you originally clicked.

## Voice settings

After selecting a voice, fine-tune its parameters:

1. Click the **Settings** icon <Icon icon="gear" iconType="solid" /> next to the voice.
2. Adjust the sliders:

<ParamField path="Stability" type="slider">
  Controls how consistent the voice sounds over time. Higher values keep tone and delivery more uniform across responses.
  
  **Recommended:** 0.7 or above for reliable performance.
</ParamField>

<ParamField path="Clarity and Similarity" type="slider">
  Adjusts how closely the generated voice matches the original recording. Higher values sound more realistic but may be less resilient to unexpected content.
</ParamField>

3. Click **Done** to save your settings.

## Separate voice management

The Voice library supports separate voice selection for:

- **Assistant voice** – The main voice your users interact with
- **Disclaimer voice** – A separate voice for legal or informational disclaimers

<Info>
If disclaimer audio is configured elsewhere, the Agent Voice page links to **Channels > Voice > Voice Configuration** for those settings.
</Info>

## Publishing changes

After selecting and configuring voices:

1. Review your changes in the Agent Voice page.
2. Click **Publish** in the top right corner to apply changes to your live agent.
