# Quicken

External target customer. PolyAI's first identified pitch target for the LLM-powered support page concept.

## What we know

- Pitch date: **Wednesday 6 May 2026.** Surfaced by [Frances Erasmus](people-frances-erasmus.md) on 2026-04-27. _Approx. 9 days from initiative kickoff — a tight prototype window._
- Frances's framing: she "thinks we can pitch it to" Quicken — i.e. fit looks promising, not yet committed.

## What we don't yet know

The Slack thread didn't capture any of the following — these are assumptions to validate before the pitch:

- Quicken's current support page setup (what's there now, what's painful for them).
- Their existing FAQ / knowledge content that would feed the agent — volume, language(s), quality.
- Their preferred languages for users — relevant to the [multilingual / translation](concept-multilingual-translation.md) capability.
- Whether they have human support agents (and a scheduling system) for the [human handoff](concept-human-handoff.md) capability.
- Commercial framing: discovery call, paid pilot, full deployment proposal?

These belong in the [Quicken pitch plan](quicken-pitch-plan.md) as work items.

## Action

Before the pitch is ready, someone needs to do an account-side discovery: what does Quicken's current support page look like, who are their users, what languages, what's the agent-readable content situation? See [Open questions](../open-questions.md).
