## What he asked for

The support page should:

- Behave like a GPT/Claude experience — prompt and converse, not search-and-link.
- Guide users to a solution and troubleshoot with them, rather than handing them an FAQ to read.
- Accept a photo or screenshot of an error from the user.
- Switch languages and translate existing content into the user's language.
- Schedule a human callback (with conversation context handed off) when it can't solve the problem.

These five capabilities, taken together, define the prototype scope. See [LLM support page](concept-llm-support-page.md) for how they decompose.
