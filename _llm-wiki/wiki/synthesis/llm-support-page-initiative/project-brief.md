# Project brief — LLM-powered support page

## What this is

A PolyAI initiative to redesign the customer support page experience around LLMs and chat, instead of the standard "search bar + FAQ cards" pattern. The support page becomes a GPT/Claude-style conversational surface that troubleshoots, accepts images, switches languages, and gracefully hands off to human agents when stuck.

This is more than a webchat widget. The chat _is_ the page. See [LLM support page](concept-llm-support-page.md) for the capability decomposition.

## Why we're doing it

Two pressures coincide:

1. **Internal:** PolyAI has [web agent technology](concept-web-agent-technology.md) that's currently being deployed in conservative ways. [Christopher Osborne](people-christopher-osborne.md) wants to push deployments further than "add a webchat widget to the site."
2. **External:** [Frances Erasmus](people-frances-erasmus.md) has identified [Quicken](quicken.md) as a concrete pitch target, with a fixed date of **Wednesday 6 May 2026.** Tight enough to force decisions; long enough to ship a credible prototype.

[Frances Erasmus](people-frances-erasmus.md) frames it commercially: support pages have been "pretty static for years" — there's room to be "super creative."
