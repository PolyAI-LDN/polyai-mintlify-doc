**Christopher Osborne — 9:00 AM**

> Team. We want to do some great deployments of our web agent tech. But not just add a webchat widget to the site. Rethink and redesign the "support page" experience. Now it's usually a search bar that surfaces FAQ pages, and some cards and links to different FAQ content below. Redesign the support page around LLMs and Chat interfaces. The support page is a GPT/Claude like experience where you prompt / converse. It doesn't just send you to an FAQ page but it guides you through to a solution and troubleshoots with you. You upload a photo or screenshot of the error you're getting. It can switch languages and translate any existing content into the language you know. Can't find a solution? It schedules a time when a human agent will call you and hands off the data.
