---
title: 'How to invite users'
description: 'Invite team members to your workspace.'
---

Add users to your workspace from **Account > Users**. Only admins can invite users.

## Steps

1. Go to **Account > Users**
2. Click **Add** (top right)
3. Enter email addresses (multiple allowed)
4. Assign role (Admin or Member)
5. Optionally assign to a group
6. Click **Confirm**
