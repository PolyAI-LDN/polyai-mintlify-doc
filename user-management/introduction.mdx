# User management

Invite team members to your PolyAI account and control their access with role-based permissions.

## Inviting users

Add colleagues to your account to collaborate on building and managing agents. Each user can be assigned a role that determines their level of access.

## Role-based access control

Assign roles to control what users can do:

- **Admin** – Full access to all features, including user management and billing
- **Editor** – Can create and modify agents, but cannot manage users or billing
- **Viewer** – Read-only access for monitoring and reviewing agent performance

## Security

User permissions are scoped to your account. Sensitive data is only accessible to authorized users based on their assigned role.
