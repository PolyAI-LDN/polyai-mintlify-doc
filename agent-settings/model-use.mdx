Choose the LLM that powers your agent. The model directly affects response quality, latency, and cost — a wrong choice can make your agent slow, expensive, or inaccurate.

<Tip>
**Recommended:** PolyAI's **Raven** model family is purpose-built for conversational AI and is the recommended choice for most deployments. Raven supports 24+ languages, delivers sub-300ms latency, and produces concise, natural responses without extensive prompt engineering. Raven V3.5 adds auto-reasoning, out-of-domain detection, and webchat support. See [Training data](/legal/training-data) for transparency on how Raven is built.
</Tip>

## Available models

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="PolyAI Raven (Recommended)" icon="brain">
    PolyAI's proprietary **Raven** model family is specialized for voice-based customer service. Trained on real customer service conversations across diverse industries, Raven eliminates the trade-off between speed and accuracy — delivering sub-300ms latency, stronger grounding, and more natural responses than general-purpose LLMs.

    | Model | Best for |
    |-------|----------|
    | **Raven V2** | Production-hardened, real-time voice interactions and high retrieval precision |
    | **Raven V3** | Multilingual support (24+ languages), improved date/time logic, conversational style built in, and stronger flow-following for enterprise voice |
    | **Raven V3.5** | All V3 capabilities plus: webchat support, auto-reasoning for complex tasks, out-of-domain detection, custom style instruction following, emotion tags for TTS, and improved safety against jailbreaks |

    **Why choose Raven:**
    - **Voice-native** — trained specifically for phone conversations, not text chat. Responses are concise and natural without extensive prompting — just add raw information to your knowledge base and Raven converts it into conversational speech
    - **Faster** — sub-300ms median latency with consistent response times. No long-tail latency spikes that disrupt conversation flow
    - **More accurate** — higher accuracy on PolyAI's voice agent benchmarks than general-purpose models, with fewer errors in function calling and knowledge base grounding
    - **Multilingual** — supports 24+ languages natively with near-perfect language consistency. Set the response language and Raven speaks it, even with English-only prompts
    - **Stronger date and time logic** — handles relative dates, scheduling scenarios, and format conversions that trip up general-purpose models
    - **Reliable function calling** — trained on real Agent Studio projects, so it calls functions with the right parameters and doesn't confuse when to respond vs. when to act
    - **Auto-reasoning** (V3.5) — automatically decides when to think deeper before responding, improving accuracy on complex tasks like date calculations without adding latency on simple turns
    - **Out-of-domain detection** (V3.5) — identifies when a user's request falls outside the agent's scope, enabling cleaner handoffs and knowledge gap tracking
    - **Safety-tuned** (V3.5) — resistant to jailbreak attempts and prompt injection, with built-in protection against hallucinations
    - **Custom style following** (V3.5) — respects custom persona and style instructions, including emotion tags, formatting rules, and channel-specific tone
    - **Optimized for Agent Studio** — understands topics, flows, and PolyAI's knowledge retrieval patterns by default. Powers the majority of PolyAI deployments

    <Note>
      Raven V2 and V3 are purpose-built for conversational voice AI and can only be selected in [Voice configuration](/voice/voice-configuration). **Raven V3.5** is also tuned for chat and can be selected in both [Voice configuration](/voice/voice-configuration) and [Chat configuration](/webchat/chat-configuration).
    </Note>

## Choosing the right model

For most use cases, **start with Raven** — it is optimized for the PolyAI platform and delivers the best out-of-the-box experience for both voice and chat. General-purpose models like GPT and Claude are trained for text-based use cases and require extensive prompting to work well for voice. Raven's voice-appropriate behavior is built in.

| Scenario | Recommended model |
|----------|-------------------|
| Voice agent (default) | **Raven V3** or **Raven V3.5** |
| Chat agent | **Raven V3.5** |
| Voice + chat (multichannel) | **Raven V3.5** for both, or Raven V3 for voice + Raven V3.5 for chat |
| Multilingual agent | **Raven V3** or **Raven V3.5** — 24+ languages with near-perfect language consistency |
| Cost-sensitive, simple tasks | GPT-5 nano or GPT-4.1 nano |
| Specific compliance requirements | Evaluate based on your provider requirements |
