## Quick overview

- **[About](./about)** - Configure your agent's greet line (first words spoken), personality (tone and vibe), and role (job function)
- **[Model](./model-use)** - Select the Large Language Model (LLM) that best suits your needs
- **[Rules](./rules)** - Define global rules governing language style, task execution, and compliance requirements
