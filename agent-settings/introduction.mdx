Configure your agent's greeting, personality, model, and rules in **Build > Agent**.

![Build > Agent overview](/images/agent-settings/agent-overview.png)

## When to configure each setting

| Setting | When to use | Impact |
|---------|------------|--------|
| **[Agent](./agent)** | Initial setup, rebranding, or changing purpose | Greeting line (first words spoken), personality (tone), and role (job function). |
| **[Model](./model-use)** | New agent setup, or optimizing for cost/latency/quality | LLM selection. [Raven](/agent-settings/raven) recommended — purpose-built for voice. Third-party models also available. |
| **[Rules](./rules)** | Consistent terminology, compliance, edge-case handling | Global constraints — tone, language style, compliance guardrails, pronunciation overrides. |
