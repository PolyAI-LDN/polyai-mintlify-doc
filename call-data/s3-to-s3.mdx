Sync call transcripts and recordings to your AWS S3 bucket for long-term storage and compliance.

## How to get started

1. [**Contact PolyAI**](https://poly.ai/contact/): Discuss your data transfer needs with your PolyAI representative.
2. **Collaborate on setup**: Partner with PolyAI's engineering team to configure, test, and validate your integration, including IAM policies and data paths.

## Related pages

If a custom AWS storage setup does not suit your use case, you can also access call recordings and transcripts using:

- [List conversations](./conversations-api/list-conversations): Programmatically retrieve conversation metadata and transcripts using the Conversations API.
- [Studio transcripts](./studio-transcripts): Review transcripts and recordings using the PolyAI platform.
