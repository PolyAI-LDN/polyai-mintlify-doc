Follow these steps to purchase phone numbers directly from our platform.

## Step-by-Step Guide

1. **Go to the agent main page**
   - Log in to your account and go to the agent main page.

2. **Find the 'Numbers' section**
   - Check the "Numbers" section on the bottom left and click "Add Number."

3. **Choose number type**
   - Select either a US or UK number.

4. **Purchase the number**
   - Click "Add Number" at the bottom of the screen.

5. **Wait for provisioning**
   - After a few seconds, you will see the number provisioned for you under the "Numbers" section.

By following these steps, you can purchase phone numbers natively in-studio. For more details, please refer to our [Support Page](/troubleshoot/support-guide) or contact our support team.
