---
title: 'Phone number integration'
description: 'Integrate a Twilio phone number with PolyAI Agent Studio'
---

## Step 1: Generate SIP in Agent Studio

1. Go to your agent page and click **Settings** in the sidebar
2. Scroll to **Connect Twilio Phone Number**
3. Click **Generate SIP** to create a SIP address for your agent

## Step 2: Configure SIP in Twilio

1. Log in to your Twilio account
2. Navigate to **Phone Numbers**
3. Select the phone number to integrate
4. Add the generated SIP trunk to the phone number

Your Twilio phone number is now integrated with your agent.
