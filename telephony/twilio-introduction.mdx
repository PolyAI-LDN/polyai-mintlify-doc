Integrating your own Twilio account with our platform allows you to leverage your existing telephony infrastructure for SMS and voice services.

## Benefits

- Use your existing Twilio numbers and SMS capabilities.
- Seamlessly integrate telephony services with your assistant.

- [How to Integrate Phone Number Hosted on Twilio](/docs/telephony-integration/twilio-integration-phone-number)
