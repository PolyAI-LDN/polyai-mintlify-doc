Connect your agent to phone numbers so callers can reach it. Manage numbers in **Configure > Numbers** — buy directly from PolyAI, integrate with Twilio, or configure outbound calling.

## Choosing your setup

How you connect phone numbers depends on your existing telephony infrastructure and what you need:

| Option | Best for | What you get |
|--------|---------|-------------|
| **[Buy a number](./how-to-buy-number)** | Quick setup, no existing telephony provider | Purchase a number directly from PolyAI — fastest path to a working agent. |
| **[Twilio integration](./twilio/introduction)** | Teams already using Twilio for voice or SMS | Connect your existing Twilio numbers and keep your current telephony stack. Supports voice, SMS, and Flex contact center routing. |
| **[Outbound calling](./outbound-calling)** | Proactive outreach (reminders, follow-ups, notifications) | Let your agent initiate calls via the API or SIP infrastructure. |

<Note>
You can also connect through other contact center platforms like [Genesys](/integrations/voice/sip/genesys), [Five9](/integrations/voice/sip/five9), or [NICE CXone](/integrations/voice/sip/NICECXone) via SIP. See [Integrations](/integrations/introduction) for the full list.
</Note>
