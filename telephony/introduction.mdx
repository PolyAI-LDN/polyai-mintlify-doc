The PolyAI Agent Studio supports both inbound and outbound calling. Phone numbers are managed from **Configure > Numbers** in the sidebar. For inbound calls, you can buy a number directly from our platform or integrate with your own Twilio account. For outbound calls, you can use the Outbound Calling API or route through your existing SIP infrastructure.
