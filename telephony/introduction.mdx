Connect phone numbers to your agent. Manage in **Configure > Numbers**.

| Option | Best for | What you get |
|--------|---------|-------------|
| **[Buy a number](./how-to-buy-number)** | No existing provider | Purchase directly from PolyAI. |
| **[Twilio integration](./twilio/introduction)** | Existing Twilio setup | Connect your Twilio numbers. Voice, SMS, and Flex routing. |
| **[Outbound calling](./outbound-calling)** | Proactive outreach | Agent-initiated calls via API or SIP. |

<Note>
You can also connect through other contact center platforms like [Genesys](/integrations/voice/sip/genesys), [Five9](/integrations/voice/sip/five9), or [NICE CXone](/integrations/voice/sip/NICECXone) via SIP. See [Integrations](/integrations/introduction) for the full list.
</Note>
