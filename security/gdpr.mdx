PolyAI complies with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) for all processing of EU personal data. This page explains what the regulation requires, how PolyAI meets each requirement, and what you need to do as a controller.

## What GDPR requires

GDPR governs how personal data of EU residents is collected, processed, stored, and transferred. Key obligations include lawful basis for processing, data minimization, storage limitation, and individual rights (access, deletion, portability).

## How PolyAI meets GDPR

| Requirement | How PolyAI addresses it |
|---|---|
| **Lawful basis** | PolyAI processes data as a data processor on your behalf. You, as the controller, determine the lawful basis for processing. |
| **Data Processing Agreement** | A DPA is available and required for GDPR compliance. Contact your account manager to execute one. |
| **Data minimization** | PolyAI processes only the data needed for the conversation. You control what data your functions and topics collect. |
| **Storage limitation** | Data retention is configurable. You can set retention periods or export data to your own storage via [S3 integration](/call-data/s3-to-s3). |
| **Right to access** | Conversation data is available via the [Conversations API](/api-reference/conversations/v3/endpoint/get-conversations) and [Studio transcripts](/call-data/studio-transcripts). |
| **Right to erasure** | Contact PolyAI support or your account manager to request data deletion for specific records. |
| **Data transfers** | PolyAI infrastructure is deployed in EU (EUW), UK, and US regions. You choose your deployment region. Cross-border transfers are covered by Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) where applicable. |
| **Breach notification** | PolyAI maintains an incident response process and will notify affected customers without undue delay in line with GDPR Article 33/34 timelines. |

## Your responsibilities as controller

- Determine and document the lawful basis for processing personal data through your agent
- Ensure callers are informed about AI-assisted call handling (via disclaimer or IVR message)
- Configure data retention periods appropriate to your use case
- Execute a DPA with PolyAI before processing EU personal data

## Request a DPA

Contact your PolyAI account manager or email [privacy@poly.ai](mailto:privacy@poly.ai) to request a Data Processing Agreement.
