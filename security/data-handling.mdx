This page documents what data PolyAI processes during conversations, where it is stored, how long it is retained, and what controls you have.

## What data is processed

During each conversation, PolyAI processes the following data types:

| Data type | When created | Contains PII | Configurable |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Audio stream** | During call (voice only) | Potentially | Region, retention |
| **Transcript** | Each turn | Potentially | Region, retention, export |
| **Conversation metadata** | Each call | No (IDs, timestamps, duration) | Region, retention, export |
| **Conversation state** (`conv.state`) | During call | Depends on your design | Region, retention |
| **Function logs** | When functions run | Depends on your design | Region, retention |
| **Audio recordings** | After call (if enabled) | Potentially | Enable/disable, retention, export |

<Note>
Whether data contains personally identifiable information (PII) depends on what your agent collects. You control this through your [managed topic](/managed-topics/introduction) content, [function](/tools/introduction) design, and [entity](/flows/no-code/entities) configuration.
</Note>

## Where data is stored

PolyAI operates in three deployment regions. Your data stays in the region you choose.

| Region | Location | API base URL |
|---|---|---|
| **US** | United States | `api.us-1.platform.polyai.app` |
| **UK** | United Kingdom | `api.uk-1.platform.polyai.app` |
| **EU West** | European Union | `api.euw-1.platform.polyai.app` |

Your deployment region is set when your project is provisioned. Contact your PolyAI account manager to confirm or change your region.

## Encryption

All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. Voice streams support SRTP encryption — see [handoff encryption options](/call-handoff/introduction) for configuration details.

For specific encryption standards and implementation details, contact your PolyAI account manager or request the security questionnaire.

## Data retention

Retention periods are configurable per deployment. Default policy:

- **Transcripts and metadata** — retained for the duration of your contract
- **Audio recordings** — retained for the duration of your contract
- **Real-time conversation context** — in-memory only, discarded at call end
- **Function logs** — retained for the duration of your contract

To request a custom retention period, contact your PolyAI account manager.

## Exporting your data

You can export conversation data to your own infrastructure:

| Method | Use case | Reference |
|---|---|---|
| **AWS S3 sync** | Automated bulk export of recordings, transcripts, and metadata | [S3 integration](/call-data/s3-to-s3) |
| **Conversations API v3** | Programmatic on-demand retrieval | [List conversations](/call-data/conversations-api/list-conversations) |
| **Webhooks** | Real-time event notifications | [Webhooks API](/api-reference/webhooks/introduction) |
| **Studio UI** | Manual review and playback | [Studio transcripts](/call-data/studio-transcripts) |

## Data deletion

To request deletion of specific conversation data:

1. Identify the affected conversations by call ID, date range, or caller number
2. Contact [PolyAI support](/troubleshoot/support-guide) or your account manager with the details
3. PolyAI will confirm deletion within your SLA timeline

For GDPR right-to-erasure requests, see [GDPR](/security/gdpr).
