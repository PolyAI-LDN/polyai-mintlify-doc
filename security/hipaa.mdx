PolyAI supports HIPAA compliance for deployments that handle protected health information (PHI). This page explains what HIPAA requires, how PolyAI meets those requirements, and how to activate HIPAA-compliant handling for your project.

## What HIPAA requires

The Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA) governs the handling of protected health information in the United States. Covered entities (healthcare providers, health plans, clearinghouses) and their business associates must implement administrative, physical, and technical safeguards to protect PHI.

## How PolyAI meets HIPAA

| Requirement | How PolyAI addresses it |
|---|---|
| **Business Associate Agreement** | A BAA is available and required before processing PHI. Contact your account manager to execute one. |
| **Access controls** | Role-based access controls in Agent Studio limit who can view transcripts, recordings, and configuration. See [user permissions](/user-management/invite-users). |
| **Encryption in transit** | All data is encrypted in transit using TLS 1.2+ for API calls and SRTP for voice streams. |
| **Encryption at rest** | Stored data (transcripts, recordings, metadata) is encrypted at rest using AES-256. |
| **Audit logging** | Platform access and configuration changes are logged for audit purposes. |
| **Data retention controls** | Retention periods are configurable per project. You can export data to your own HIPAA-compliant storage via [S3 integration](/call-data/s3-to-s3). |
| **Minimum necessary** | PolyAI processes only the data needed for the conversation. You control what PHI your agent collects through topic content and function design. |

## Activating HIPAA-compliant handling

<Steps>
  <Step title="Execute a BAA">
    Contact your PolyAI account manager to execute a Business Associate Agreement before your agent handles PHI.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose your deployment region">
    Select a deployment region that meets your data residency requirements. See [regional endpoints](/api-reference/introduction#base-urls).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure data retention">
    Set retention periods appropriate for your compliance needs. Configure [S3 export](/call-data/s3-to-s3) for long-term archival.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Design your agent with PHI in mind">
    Minimize the PHI your agent collects. Use [managed topics](/managed-topics/introduction) and [rules](/agent-settings/rules) to control what the agent asks for and stores.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure access controls">
    Restrict access to transcripts and recordings using [role-based permissions](/user-management/invite-users).
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Request a BAA

Contact your PolyAI account manager or email [privacy@poly.ai](mailto:privacy@poly.ai) to request a Business Associate Agreement.
