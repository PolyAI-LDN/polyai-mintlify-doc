PolyAI supports HIPAA compliance for deployments that handle protected health information (PHI). This page explains what HIPAA requires and how PolyAI supports your compliance.

## How PolyAI supports HIPAA compliance

| Requirement | How PolyAI addresses it |
|---|---|
| **Business Associate Agreement** | A BAA is available for customers who process PHI. Contact your account manager. |
| **Access controls** | Role-based access controls in Agent Studio limit who can view transcripts, recordings, and configuration. See [user permissions](/user-management/invite-users). |
| **Encryption** | Data is encrypted in transit and at rest. Voice streams support SRTP — see [encryption options](/call-handoff/introduction). |
| **Data retention controls** | Retention periods are configurable per project. You can export data to your own storage via [S3 integration](/call-data/s3-to-s3). |
| **Minimum necessary** | PolyAI processes only the data needed for the conversation. You control what PHI your agent collects through topic content and function design. |

## Activating HIPAA-compliant handling

<Steps>
  <Step title="Execute a BAA">
    Contact your PolyAI account manager to execute a Business Associate Agreement before your agent handles PHI.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose your deployment region">
    Select a deployment region that meets your data residency requirements. See [regional endpoints](/api-reference/introduction#base-urls).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure data retention">
    Set retention periods appropriate for your compliance needs. Configure [S3 export](/call-data/s3-to-s3) for long-term archival.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Design your agent with PHI in mind">
    Minimize the PHI your agent collects. Use [managed topics](/managed-topics/introduction) and [rules](/agent-settings/rules) to control what the agent asks for and stores.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure access controls">
    Restrict access to transcripts and recordings using [role-based permissions](/user-management/invite-users).
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Request a BAA

Contact your PolyAI account manager to request a Business Associate Agreement.
