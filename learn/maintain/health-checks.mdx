Regular health checks help you catch issues before they impact customers.

<Tip>
[Smart Analyst](/smart-analyst/introduction) can accelerate every routine below. Its **deep sampling** analyzes up to 500 conversations per query — use it to surface patterns, identify failure reasons, and prioritize fixes without manually reviewing calls.
</Tip>

## Schedule overview

| Routine | Frequency | Time required | What to check |
|---------|-----------|---------------|---------------|
| Daily check | Every day | 10-15 min | Dashboards, recent errors, handoff rate |
| Weekly review | Every week | 30-60 min | Trends, unhandled queries, sample calls, test sets |
| Monthly deep dive | Every month | 2-4 hours | Month-over-month metrics, knowledge audit, function optimization |
| Pre-deployment | Before each promotion | 20-30 min | Test sets, manual testing, integration checks |
| Post-deployment | After each promotion | 30 min | Live calls, key metrics, function logs |

## Monitoring routines

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Daily" icon="sun">
    **Time:** 10-15 minutes each morning

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Review the Standard dashboard">
        Check call volume, handoff rate, and latency against your baseline in the [Standard dashboard](/analytics/dashboards/standard).
      </Step>
      <Step title="Scan recent errors">
        Go to **Analytics > Conversations** filtered to last 24 hours.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Spot-check handoff reasons">
        Look for new patterns — or ask [Smart Analyst](/smart-analyst/introduction): *"What are the top handoff reasons from the last 24 hours?"*
      </Step>
      <Step title="Verify integrations">
        Look for API errors in function logs.
      </Step>
    </Steps>

    <Warning>
      **Red flags — stop and investigate if you see:**
      - Handoff rate > 50% (or 20% above baseline)
      - Average latency > 3 seconds
      - Error rate > 5%
      - Call volume drop > 30%
    </Warning>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Weekly" icon="calendar-week">
    **Time:** 30-60 minutes each week

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Compare metrics">
        Compare this week's call volume, containment, duration, and latency to last week.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Review unhandled queries">
        Check dashboards and prioritize knowledge gaps.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Run Smart Analyst deep sampling">
        Use [Smart Analyst](/smart-analyst/introduction) to surface trends — e.g., *"What are the top 5 reasons calls are handed off this week?"*
      </Step>
      <Step title="Listen to flagged calls">
        Review 5-10 calls flagged by Smart Analyst (mix of successful and unsuccessful).
      </Step>
      <Step title="Check test set results">
        Run [test sets](/analytics/test-suite/introduction) for regressions.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Plan improvements">
        Prioritize fixes based on Smart Analyst insights and test results.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Monthly" icon="calendar">
    **Time:** 2-4 hours at month end

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Compare key metrics month-over-month">
        Review all core metrics against the previous month.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Run comprehensive Smart Analyst analysis">
        Use [Smart Analyst](/smart-analyst/introduction) deep sampling to break down containment by handoff reason, identify recurring failures, and surface sentiment trends. Try: *"Analyze the top transfer reasons and containment blockers over the last 30 days with percentage breakdowns."*
      </Step>
      <Step title="Audit Managed Topics">
        Review all [Managed Topics](/managed-topics/introduction) for outdated content — use Smart Analyst to identify knowledge gaps: *"What questions are we not handling well?"*
      </Step>
      <Step title="Review function performance">
        Optimize or refactor slow functions.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Maintain test sets">
        Add new scenarios and remove obsolete ones from [test sets](/analytics/test-suite/introduction).
      </Step>
      <Step title="Document changes">
        Review [version history](/environments-and-versions/project-history) and document major changes.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Deployment checks

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Pre-deployment" icon="clipboard-check">
    Before promoting any version to Pre-release or Live:

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Run all test sets">
        Investigate any failures before promoting.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Test critical journeys">
        Manually test critical user journeys and edge cases.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Test integrations">
        Verify all external API integrations are working.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Check voice quality">
        Test voice quality and pronunciations.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Compare versions">
        Use [diffs](/environments-and-versions/diffs) to compare against the current Live version.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Prepare rollback plan">
        Identify the last known good version.
      </Step>
    </Steps>

    <Check>**Promote if:** All tests pass, no critical bugs, performance is acceptable.</Check>
    <Warning>**Do not promote if:** Test failures exist, performance degraded, or integrations are failing.</Warning>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Post-deployment" icon="radar">
    After promoting to Pre-release or Live:

    **First 30 minutes:**
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Watch conversations in real time">
        Monitor [Conversation Review](/analytics/conversations/review) for issues.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Monitor key metrics">
        Watch latency, error rate, and handoff rate.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Check function logs">
        Verify no new errors in function logs.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Be ready to rollback">
        Keep the rollback version identified and ready.
      </Step>
    </Steps>

    **First 24 hours:** Check metrics every 2-4 hours against baseline. Review handoff reasons for new patterns.

    **First week:** Analyze full week of data vs. pre-deployment baseline. Document lessons learned.

    <Warning>
      **Rollback immediately if:**
      - Error rate > 10%
      - Handoff rate doubles
      - Critical function failures
      - Customer complaints spike
    </Warning>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Tips

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Start small" icon="seedling">
    If you can't do everything, prioritize daily checks and pre-deployment checks — they have the highest ROI.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Let Smart Analyst do the heavy lifting" icon="magnifying-glass-chart">
    Instead of manually reviewing calls, use [Smart Analyst deep sampling](/smart-analyst/introduction) to analyze hundreds of conversations in minutes. It's especially effective for weekly and monthly reviews where you need to spot patterns across large volumes of data.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Automate where possible" icon="robot">
    Use [test sets](/analytics/test-suite/introduction) for regression testing and the [Alerts API](/api-reference/alerts/introduction) for anomaly notifications.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Adjust frequency to match your needs" icon="sliders">
    High-volume or mission-critical agents need tighter monitoring; stable agents can relax the schedule.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
