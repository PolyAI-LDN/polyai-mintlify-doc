This page covers how to update voice settings, manage audio quality, and fix common audio issues.

## Quick reference

| I need to... | Action |
|---|---|
| Change the agent's voice | **Channels > Voice > Agent Voice** → Change |
| Adjust voice parameters | **Channels > Voice > Agent Voice** → settings gear |
| Fix a mispronunciation | **Channels > Voice > Response Control** → Pronunciations |
| Update cached audio | **Audio Management** → Edit → Sync |
| Change interaction style | **Channels > Voice > Audio Management** → Interaction style |
| Enable/disable barge-in | **Channels > Voice > Audio Management** → toggle |
| Upload custom audio | **Audio Management** → Upload |

## Changing your agent's voice

Consider updating when your brand refreshes, customers report clarity issues, you're expanding to new languages, or newer voice models become available.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Agent Voice settings">
    Go to **Channels > Voice > Agent Voice**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Browse the Voice Library">
    Click **Change** to open the [Voice Library](/voice/voice-library). Filter by **Language**, **Region**, and **Gender**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Preview and select">
    Preview voices with custom text, then click **Select** to apply.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Test before publishing">
    Test in Agent Chat before publishing.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>For non-English projects, use a `multilingual_v2` model to ensure proper language support.</Tip>

For programmatic voice configuration, see [voice classes](/tools/classes/voice) and [Add a voice](/voice/add-a-new-voice).

## Interaction style and barge-in

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Interaction style" icon="gauge-high">
    Control how quickly your agent responds in **Channels > Voice > Audio Management**:

    | Mode | Delay | Best for |
    |---|---|---|
    | **Turbo** | 400ms | Ultra-fast, may interrupt more |
    | **Swift** | 1200ms | Simple queries |
    | **Balanced** | 1600ms | Most use cases (default) |
    | **Precise** | 2000ms | Complex queries needing accuracy |
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Barge-in" icon="hand">
    Toggle in **Channels > Voice > Audio Management**. Lets callers interrupt the agent mid-sentence.

    <Check>**Enable when:** using Turbo mode, callers frequently interrupt, or you want more natural conversations.</Check>
    <Warning>**Disable when:** delivering complete information (legal disclaimers), or background noise causes false interruptions.</Warning>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Managing audio quality

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Cached audio" icon="database" defaultOpen={true}>
    The Audio Management tab lets you cache and optimize frequently-used audio for reduced latency and consistent quality.

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Find the utterance">Go to **Channels > Voice > Audio Management**.</Step>
      <Step title="Edit settings">Click **Edit** to adjust stability/clarity settings or add IPA pronunciation corrections.</Step>
      <Step title="Regenerate and preview">Click the **sync** icon to regenerate, then preview the result.</Step>
    </Steps>

    <Tip>Audio is only cached after the same TTS is generated at least twice within 24 hours. For critical phrases (greetings, transfers), generate them repeatedly or upload manually.</Tip>
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Custom audio uploads" icon="upload">
    Upload pre-recorded audio (WAV or MP3) for maximum control over greetings, legal disclaimers, or brand-specific moments.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Fixing pronunciations

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Pronunciations">
    Go to **Channels > Voice > Response Control** → **Pronunciations** tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add a pronunciation">
    Click **Add pronunciation** and enter the word as it appears in text.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Provide the correct pronunciation">
    Provide the IPA pronunciation (e.g., "PolyAI" → `/ˈpɒli eɪ aɪ/`).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Test">
    Test in Agent Chat.
  </Step>
</Steps>

You can also use SSML for advanced control:

```xml
<break time="500ms"/>
<prosody rate="slow">Speak this slowly</prosody>
```

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Voice sounds robotic" icon="robot">
    **Likely cause:** Low-quality TTS

    **Fix:** Switch to Cartesia or ElevenLabs.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Agent speaks too fast" icon="forward-fast">
    **Likely cause:** Rate set too high

    **Fix:** Adjust via settings gear in Agent Voice.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Agent interrupts frequently" icon="comment-exclamation">
    **Likely cause:** Turbo mode without barge-in

    **Fix:** Enable barge-in or switch to Balanced.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Mispronunciations" icon="volume-xmark">
    **Likely cause:** TTS doesn't recognize word

    **Fix:** Add pronunciation in Response Control.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="High latency on audio" icon="hourglass-half">
    **Likely cause:** Slow TTS provider

    **Fix:** Switch to Cartesia or use cached audio.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Background noise causes interruptions" icon="waveform-lines">
    **Likely cause:** Barge-in too sensitive

    **Fix:** Disable barge-in or adjust interaction style.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Maintenance routine

- **Monthly:** Listen to recent calls and identify voice quality issues
- **As needed:** Add pronunciations for new terms
- **After voice changes:** Regenerate cached audio
