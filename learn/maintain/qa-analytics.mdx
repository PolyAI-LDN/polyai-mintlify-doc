Use **Dashboards** for metrics, **Conversation review** for transcript inspection, and **[Smart Analyst](/smart-analyst/introduction)** for AI-powered analysis at scale.

<Tip>
**Skip manual call reviews with Smart Analyst.** Instead of listening to individual calls, use [Smart Analyst deep sampling](/smart-analyst/introduction) to analyze up to 500 conversations per query. Ask questions like *"What are the top handoff reasons this week?"* and get structured insights in minutes.
</Tip>

## Quick reference

| I need to... | Action | Time estimate |
|--------------|--------|---------------|
| Check daily performance | Configure > Dashboards → Standard → Review key metrics | 5 min |
| Listen to recent calls | Analytics > Conversations → Filter → Listen | 10-20 min |
| Find knowledge gaps | Configure > Dashboards → Unhandled queries | 10 min |
| Analyze trends across hundreds of calls | Analytics > [Smart Analyst](/smart-analyst/introduction) → Ask a question | 5 min |
| Review a specific topic | Analytics > Conversations → Filter by topic | 5 min |
| Export call data | Analytics > Conversations → Export CSV/JSON | 5 min |
| Tag problematic calls | Open call → Add category/annotation | 2 min |
| Check containment rate | Configure > Dashboards → Standard → Containment metric | 2 min |
| Review safety issues | Safety dashboard → Check flags | 10 min |

## Example: did my FAQ edit improve containment?

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Standard dashboard">
    Navigate to **Configure > Dashboards > Standard**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set the date range">
    Set **From** to the day you promoted the change, and **To** to today.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check containment rate">
    - If it went up → good sign
    - If it dropped or stayed flat → drill down to conversations (next section)
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Review conversations

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Conversations">
    Go to **Analytics > Conversations**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Apply filters">
    - **Date range** — same window you used in the dashboard
    - **Topic** — select the FAQ you edited
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open a transcript">
    Click a call to open the transcript.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check the result">
    - Did the agent serve the new answer?
    - Did the caller stay in self-service or ask for a human?
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tag if needed">
    If something looks off, tag it for follow-up.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Tagging and annotations

At the bottom-right of a transcript:

- **Category** — pick a label like *Needs KB fix* or *Escalated unnecessarily*
- **Annotation** — flag *Wrong transcription* or *Missing topic*

These tags surface in QA reports and help you spot patterns over time.

## Export transcripts (optional)

<Steps>
  <Step title="Set your filters">In **Conversations**, select your filters.</Step>
  <Step title="Export">Click **Export** → *CSV* or *JSON*.</Step>
  <Step title="Download">You'll get a download link with call metadata and the full transcripts.</Step>
</Steps>

See [Call data → Transcripts](/call-data/studio-transcripts) for field definitions.

## Common workflows

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Daily check" icon="sun">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Open Standard dashboard">Review key metrics vs. baseline.</Step>
      <Step title="Check call volume and containment rate">Look for anomalies.</Step>
      <Step title="Review average handle time and handoff rate">Compare against expected ranges.</Step>
      <Step title="Drill into Conversation Review if needed">Investigate any anomalies.</Step>
    </Steps>

    **Time:** 5-10 minutes
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Weekly quality review" icon="calendar-week">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Start with Smart Analyst">
        Run a deep sampling query: *"What are the top issues and failure reasons from the last 7 days?"*
      </Step>
      <Step title="Review findings">
        Identify areas that need manual review.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Listen to sample calls">
        Review 5-10 calls flagged by Smart Analyst or filtered in Conversation Review.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Tag issues for follow-up">
        Categorize and annotate problematic calls.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Update knowledge or settings">
        Apply fixes based on insights.
      </Step>
    </Steps>

    **Time:** 20-40 minutes (Smart Analyst reduces manual review time)
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Investigating handoff spikes" icon="chart-line-up">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Identify the spike">
        Go to **Configure > Dashboards** → Standard dashboard. Find when handoff rate increased.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Ask Smart Analyst">
        *"What are the top reasons for handoffs in the last 3 days? Give me percentage breakdowns."*
      </Step>
      <Step title="Validate findings">
        Review specific conversations in **Analytics > Conversations** to confirm.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Apply fixes">
        Add missing knowledge or adjust handoff rules based on the patterns identified.
      </Step>
    </Steps>

    **Time:** 15-20 minutes
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
