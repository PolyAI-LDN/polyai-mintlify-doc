## 9. Select voice

[ ] Choose a voice that matches the agent’s role and audience.

Voice choice strongly affects trust and perceived competence.

**Do**
- Go to **Configure → Agent Voice**.
- Select voices for:
  - Assistant
  - Disclaimer (if required)
- Adjust settings:
  - **Stability**: aim for 0.7 or higher for reliability.
  - **Clarity / similarity**: tune realism versus robustness.
- Preview common utterances:
  - Greeting
  - SMS offer
  - Handoff language
  - Closing line
- Publish changes.

**Verify**
- The voice sounds natural and consistent.
- It matches the intended tone of the agent.
- Updated audio is audible in Call testing.
