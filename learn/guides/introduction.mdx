## Structure

<Note>
  Guides are structured, goal-driven walkthroughs. Each one is designed to produce a concrete, verifiable outcome.
</Note>

PolyAcademy is your self-directed, guided learning experience.

Each level is an entirely self-contained and self-paced learning experience, designed to expand upon your existing knowledge and elevate your skills to increasing platform competency.

Every level consists of:

1. **<u>Introduction:</u>** Introducing you to the module and what we'll cover, and key learning outcomes
2. **<u>Modules:</u>** taking you through the core topics needed for you current level of skill and expertise
3. **<u>Practical:</u>** Your final Agent building experience for that level. This is your opportunity to put your skills to the test and combine everything you've learnt to build an agent. Don't worry, there'll be hints and tips to support you through and you can always refer back to the other modules at any time.
4. **<u>Knowledge Check:</u>** Time to put your knowledge to the test! Here you can assess what you've remembered and what you might want to brush up on before advancing to the next level

Each module consists of:

1. **_Written and picture content_** - giving you the practical and theoretical knowledge you need to operate Agent Studio and build, deploy and analyse agents for your level.
2. **_Bite sized practical tasks with follow-along video content_** - allowing you to learn the platform through hands-on, practical walkthroughs
3. **_AS Playground_** - A dedicated, pressure free environment to practice your agent building skills for that module and walkthrough
