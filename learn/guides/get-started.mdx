Welcome to **PolyAcademy** level one, where you can create a complete, working deployment you can test in both web **Chat** and
using voice **Call**. We will review the **Conversation review**, and how to safely promote a draft to a published agent.

Follow the completion checklist steps in order:

You are done with **level one** of the PolyAcademy when all of the following are complete:

1. [You have created your project](/learn/guides/get-started/create-a-project)
2. [You have customized your agent](/learn/guides/get-started/edit-agent-behavior)
3. [A simple FAQ knowledge base topic works (and you can test it)](/learn/guides/get-started/add-kb-topic)
4. [More complex knowledge base topics like an SMS with a handoff works](/learn/guides/get-started/add-complex-kb-topic)
5. [You have figured out any necessary response controls](/learn/guides/get-started/response-control)
6. [You are happy with your agent's voice](/learn/guides/get-started/edit-voice-settings)
7. [The audio cache is understood and managed](/learn/guides/get-started/audio-management)
8. [Any global ASR biasing or corrections are configured](/learn/guides/get-started/global-asr)
9. [Changes are tested and versioned in environments](/learn/guides/get-started/environments)
10. [Previous conversations are viewable in Conversation review](/learn/guides/get-started/conv-review)
11. [Variants are configured](/learn/guides/get-started/variants)
