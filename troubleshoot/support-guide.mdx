Most issues can be resolved without contacting support. Start with the steps below, and reach out if you still need help.

## Troubleshoot first

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check common issues">
    Review the [common issues](/learn/maintain/common-issues) table for quick fixes to frequent problems like missing edits, failed handoffs, or broken imports.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review the FAQ">
    The [FAQ](/troubleshoot/faq) covers questions about topics, rules, actions, and agent configuration.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Diagnose the conversation">
    Use [conversation diagnosis](/analytics/conversations/diagnosis) to inspect function calls, flows, topic citations, and turn latency for a specific call.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check your environment">
    Confirm you're looking at the right [environment](/environments-and-versions/introduction). Changes saved in Sandbox aren't live until promoted.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Contact support

If the steps above don't resolve your issue, email the PolyAI support team at **[support@poly.ai](mailto:support@poly.ai)**.

<Card title="Email support" icon="envelope" href="mailto:support@poly.ai?subject=Support%20request%3A%20%5BBrief%20description%5D&body=Project%20link%3A%20%0AEnvironment%3A%20%5BSandbox%20%2F%20Pre-release%20%2F%20Live%5D%0ACall%20ID%20(if%20applicable)%3A%20%0A%0AWhat%20happened%3A%0A%0AWhat%20I%20expected%3A%0A%0ASteps%20to%20reproduce%3A%0A1.%20%0A2.%20%0A3.%20">
  Open a pre-filled email to support@poly.ai with the details the team needs to help you.
</Card>

Include the following in your message:

- **Project link** — a URL to the relevant project in Studio
- **Environment** — Sandbox, Pre-release, or Live
- **Call ID** — from the [Conversations](/analytics/conversations/review) page, if applicable
- **Description** — what you expected vs. what happened
- **Steps to reproduce** — how to trigger the issue

<Tip>
Including a call ID helps the support team investigate faster.
</Tip>

## Report a bug

If you believe you've found a product bug:

1. Reproduce the issue and capture screenshots or relevant logs.
2. Note your browser (e.g. Chrome, Safari) and environment (Sandbox, Pre-release, or Live).
3. Email [support@poly.ai](mailto:support@poly.ai) with the details above.

<Warning>
Always test in [Sandbox](/environments-and-versions/introduction) before reporting issues found in production. Some problems are caused by unpromoted changes.
</Warning>
