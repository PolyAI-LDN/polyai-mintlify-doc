The **Metrics** page (**Configure > Metrics**) lets you define custom metrics that track specific aspects of your agent's performance.

## Overview

Custom metrics allow you to measure outcomes beyond the standard analytics. Define what success looks like for your use case and track it across conversations.

## Defining metrics

1. Go to **Configure > Metrics** in the sidebar.
2. Create a new metric by specifying a **name** and **definition**.
3. Configure how the metric is calculated or triggered.

<Note>
Custom metrics can be referenced in [Dashboards](/analytics/dashboards/introduction) and used alongside standard analytics.
</Note>
