## What free trial mode does

Free trial mode is a streamlined view of the docs for self-serve Agent Studio users on the **60-day free trial**. Enterprise features, developer tools, and advanced configuration stay visible in the sidebar but are greyed out and badged **Enterprise**, so you can see what exists and what isn't part of the free trial without losing your bearings.

For a feature-by-feature breakdown of what the free trial includes, see [Open Platform](/platform/open-platform).

The same upsell pattern applies inside Agent Studio. When you try to connect an enterprise-only integration, the product shows you why it's gated and how to talk to sales:

![Integration Restricted modal in Agent Studio: Twilio is not enabled on trial accounts, with Close and Contact Sales buttons](/images/platform/enterprise-restricted-modal.png)

## How to exit

You can leave free trial mode at any time in three ways:

- Click the **Free trial – exit** pill floating in the top-right corner of any docs page.
- If you land on an enterprise page in free trial mode, click **Exit free trial mode** in the upsell banner at the top of that page.
- Come back to this page and click **Exit free trial mode**.

## Who is this for?

<Tip>
  **Use free trial mode if you are...**
  - Signed up at [studio.us.poly.ai](https://studio.us.poly.ai) and exploring the self-serve plan.
  - Building your first agent through Studio Assistant and the Agent Studio UI.
  - Looking for knowledge, voice, Polyphone, and analytics docs without enterprise-only or code noise.
</Tip>

<Note>
  **Use full docs if you are...**
  - On an Enterprise plan and need access to telephony, SMS, handoffs, custom metrics, or dashboards.
  - Writing Python tools or secrets.
  - Integrating through the ADK or REST APIs.
  - Configuring advanced flows, variant management, or custom telephony.
</Note>
