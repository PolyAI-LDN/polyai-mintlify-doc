The **Portal Transcripts** feature provides a straightforward way to access and review detailed transcripts of customer interactions directly within the PolyAI platform. Designed for compliance, quality assurance, and operational insights, this tool enables you to analyze historical conversations and ensure transparency in customer interactions.

For programmatic access to transcripts, refer to [List Conversations](list-conversations).

---

## Accessing Transcripts in the PolyAI Portal

To view conversation transcripts, follow these steps:

1. Navigate to the **Conversations Log** in the PolyAI Portal.
2. Use the **Filters** option to search for specific conversations. Filters include:
   - **Time Range**: Specify start and end times to narrow your search.
   - **Call Details**: Search by call number or customer identifier.
   - **Conversation ID (Call SID)**: Use the unique system-generated identifier for precision.
3. Select a conversation to view its full transcript.

### Transcript Details

Each transcript includes:
- **Metadata**:
  - Conversation start and end times, along with total duration.
  - Unique Call SID for identifying the interaction.
  - Agent or assistant names associated with the call.
- **Speaker Segmentation**:
  - Clear differentiation between customer and assistant speech, including timestamps for each utterance.
- **Handoff Information**:
  - Details of live agent transitions, such as handoff state, agent ID, and timestamps.

## Use Cases for Portal Transcripts

### Compliance

Organizations can retain and review conversation transcripts to meet regulatory standards such as HIPAA or GDPR. Transcripts provide a clear record of customer interactions, ensuring adherence to policies and demonstrating compliance during audits.

### Quality Assurance

Use transcripts to evaluate assistant performance and identify areas for improvement. Monitor handoffs to live agents to ensure they are handled smoothly, minimizing customer friction and maintaining service standards.

### Operational Insights

Analyze transcripts to uncover trends, customer sentiment, and resolution outcomes. Combine this data with conversation metadata to generate actionable insights, such as identifying patterns in customer issues or refining training datasets.

## Best Practices

1. **Use Filters Efficiently**: Apply relevant filters (time-based, state-based, or by Call SID) to quickly locate specific conversations.
2. **Review Handoff Events**: Focus on transcripts involving live agent handoffs to ensure smooth transitions and consistent experiences.
3. **Leverage API Access for Bulk Data**: For larger-scale analysis or integrations, combine the Portal Transcripts feature with the [List Conversations](list-conversations) API for bulk access.
