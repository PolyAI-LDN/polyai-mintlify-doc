---
title: 'Quickstart'
description: 'Follow this guide to prepare your assistant for testing'
---

<Info>
  Before you begin, make sure you've signed up for an account. You can find detailed instructions [here](/get-started/account-creation).
</Info>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Start the creation process">
    After logging in, click **“Create Assistant”** to start the process.

    ![create-assistant](/images/02-02-01-01-create-assistant.png)

    <Info>
      You can also **duplicate** an existing assistant from the home page by clicking the three dots information icon next to a profile.
       ![duplicate-assistant](/images/duplicate-assistant.png)
       The duplicated profile will have the same name as the original, but appended with `(copy)`. You can also export and import existing assistants as `.bin` files.
    </Info>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set a name, response language, voice, and greeting line">
    Full page: [**Voice**](/voice/introduction), [**Agent settings**](/agent-settings/introduction)

    Set the general information for the assistant, including **assistant name**, **response language**, **voice**, and **the welcome greeting**.

    <Info>
      You can also set a custom disclaimer message to precede the call using an alternate voice.
    </Info>

    After completing these steps, click **"Next"**.

    ![general](/images/02-02-01-02-general.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select personality, role, and additional info">
    Next, select the **personality**, **role**, and enter the **additional info** for your assistant.

    <Tip>
      Any additional info will be included as part of your assistant's personality prompt, packaged together with the other fields in this section.
    </Tip>

    After completing these steps, click **"Next"**.

    ![about](/images/02-02-01-03-about.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select knowledge topics">
    Full page: [**Knowledge base**](/knowledge-base/introduction)

    Knowledge refers to the information and context the assistant will have access to.

    ![knowledge](/images/02-02-01-04-knowledge.png)

    Once you have completed your knowledge topics, you are ready to publish your assistant to the sandbox. For more information, visit the [versioning overview](/environments-and-versions/introduction).
  </Step>

  <Step title="[Optional] Select a number for telephony integration">
    Full page: [**Telephony Integration**](/telephony/introduction)

    You may buy a number or connect an existing number for your assistant. You can also skip this step and return later.

    ![telephony](/images/02-02-01-05-telephony.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="[Advanced] Integrate functions into your knowledge base">
    Full page: [**Functions**](/function/introduction)

    **What they do**: Functions allow you to add dynamic, programmable behavior to your assistant, and allow you to handle external API calls, retrieve specific data, and use that data make decisions based on real-time context.

    Example use case: An assistant retrieving shipping information for a customer query about an order.

    ![functions](/images/quickstart-functions.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="[Advanced] Add a flow to your project">
    Full page: [**Flows**](/flows/introduction)

    **What they do**: Flows are designed for managing multi-step conversations, like booking a table at a restaurant, resetting a password, or collecting feedback.

    Example use case: A customer reservation flow for a restaurant, with steps to ask for the date, time, and number of people before confirming the booking, as well as handling any problems that might arise along the way.

    ![flows](/images/quickstart-flows.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>

By following these steps, you can create a personalized and effective assistant tailored to your needs.
