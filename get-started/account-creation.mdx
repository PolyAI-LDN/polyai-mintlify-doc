title: 'Account creation'
description: 'Sign up for PolyAI with Google SSO or email to start building agents.'
---

Sign up for your PolyAI account to access Agent Studio and begin building AI agents. You can authenticate using Google SSO or create a password-based account.

<Frame caption="PolyAI sign-up interface">
  ![overview](/images/get-started/overview.png)
</Frame>

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Google SSO">
    Sign in with your Google account. No separate password required.

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Open the sign-up page">
        Go to the **sign-up page**: [https://studio.us.poly.ai/](https://studio.us.poly.ai/).

        ![sign-up-page](/images/get-started/sign-up-page.png)
      </Step>
      <Step title="Select Google SSO">
        Click **"Sign up with Google"**.

        ![google-sso](/images/get-started/select-google-sso.png)
      </Step>
      <Step title="Sign in with Google">
        A Google sign-in window will appear. Select your Google account or enter your Google email and password.

        ![sign-in-with-google](/images/get-started/sign-in-with-google.png)
      </Step>
      <Step title="Authorize">
        Allow PolyAI to access your Google account by clicking **"Continue"**.

        ![authorize-agent-studio](/images/get-started/authorize-jupiter.png)
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Email and password">
    Use any email address and a password you manage yourself.

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Open the sign-up page">
        Go to the **sign-up page**: [https://studio.us.poly.ai/](https://studio.us.poly.ai/).

        ![sign-up-page](/images/get-started/sign-up.png)
      </Step>
      <Step title="Enter your details">
        Fill in your **first name**, **last name**, and **email address**.

        Create a **password** that meets the following requirements:

        <Info>
          **Password requirements:**
          - At least 12 characters in length
          - Contains at least 3 of the following 4 character types: lower case letters (a-z), upper case letters (A-Z), numbers (0-9), special characters (!@#$%^&*)
          - No more than 2 identical characters in a row (e.g., "aaa" not allowed)
        </Info>

        ![enter-your-details](/images/get-started/enter-your-details.png)
      </Step>
      <Step title="Verify your email">
        Click **"Create account"** to complete the process. Check your email for a verification link and click **"Verify account"**.

        ![verify-your-email](/images/get-started/verify-your-email.png)
      </Step>
      <Step title="Complete registration">
        Once your email is verified, you can log in to your new account.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Tip>
  Once signed in, follow the [quickstart](/get-started/quickstart) to create your first agent.
</Tip>
