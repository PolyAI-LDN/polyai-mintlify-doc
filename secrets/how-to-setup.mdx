## Create a secret

1. Navigate to the **Secrets** tab on the account landing page
2. Click **Add secret** in the top right corner
3. Fill in the details:
   - **Name** - The secret name
   - **Description** (optional) - Explain its purpose
   - **Value** - Store as a single value (string) or key/value pairs (dictionary)
4. Under **Agent access**, select which agents can retrieve this secret
5. Click **Add**

<Note>Agents without access granted here cannot retrieve the secret.</Note>
