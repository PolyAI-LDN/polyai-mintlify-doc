# Secrets

Welcome to the Secrets Vault, a powerful feature of our platform designed to securely store API keys, tokens, credentials, and other sensitive information. With the Secrets Vault, you can easily manage and access your secrets, empowering you to integrate with your ecosystem and enhance the capabilities of your agents.

## Importance

At PolyAI, we understand the importance of integrating with your existing systems and services. The Secrets Vault enables you to securely store sensitive information and impose access control per secret, providing an additional layer of security and control over your data. By using the Secrets Vault, you can create powerful function calls within your agents, enhancing their capabilities and delivering an exceptional experience to your users.
