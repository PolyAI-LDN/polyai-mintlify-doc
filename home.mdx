PolyAI builds AI agents for real customer conversations — voice and chat. Agents answer questions, make bookings, resolve issues, and hand off to humans when needed. Most run on [Raven](/agent-settings/raven), PolyAI's proprietary LLM with sub-300ms latency across 24+ languages.

Build, deploy, and monitor agents through [Agent Studio](/get-started/introduction), the [ADK](/extend/adk), or [REST APIs](/api-reference/introduction).
