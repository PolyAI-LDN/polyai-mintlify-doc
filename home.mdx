PolyAI builds AI agents for real customer conversations — voice and chat. Agents answer questions, make bookings, resolve issues, and hand off to humans when needed. Most run on [Raven](/agent-settings/raven), PolyAI's proprietary LLM with sub-300ms latency across 24+ languages.

Build, deploy, and monitor agents through [Agent Studio](/get-started/introduction), the [ADK](/extend/adk), or [REST APIs](/api-reference/introduction).

## Welcome

You've signed up at [studio.poly.ai](https://studio.poly.ai/) for the Open platform – the same Agent Studio that runs enterprise voice AI at scale, opened up for self-serve. Below is the path from sign-up to a shareable voice agent. The whole loop fits inside 10 minutes for a first agent.

## The Open platform workflow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Describe your agent in Studio Assistant">
    Open [Studio Assistant](/studio-assistant/introduction) – it's the homepage when you log in. Describe what you want the agent to do in natural language, drop in a company URL or a document, and the assistant scaffolds a working voice agent: flows, knowledge base, voice, and integrations. Nothing is written until you approve the plan.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Talk to your agent in the browser">
    Every agent gets a [shareable PolyPhone link](/polyphone/introduction) – a WebRTC call you can place from any browser. No telephony, no app to install. Use it to test the agent yourself, demo it to colleagues, or share it for feedback.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Embed the PolyPhone widget on your site">
    Drop a single `<script>` tag on any page on your website. Visitors click a launcher in the corner and they're talking to your agent. The widget is voice-only on the Open platform; webchat is on the enterprise tier.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review conversations and iterate">
    Every transcript and recording is in **Analytics > Conversations**. The standard dashboard tracks total conversations, containment, PolyScore overview, cost savings, and handle time. Use Studio Assistant in **Ask** mode to query your agent's behaviour without leaving the home screen.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Scale to enterprise when you're ready">
    Outgrown self-serve? Use the [ADK](/extend/adk) to `poly pull` your project and `poly push` it onto an enterprise cluster, where telephony, real-time configuration, SMS, handoffs, and the full analytics suite live. [Request a demo](https://poly.ai/request-a-demo) to talk about a contract.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Start from a template

Skip the blank page. Pick a starter prompt below, paste it into Studio Assistant, and let it scaffold the agent. Edit anything you don't like once it's built.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Restaurant reservations" icon="utensils">
    *"Build a reservations agent for a restaurant. Greet the caller, take party size, date and time, name, and phone number, then confirm. If the slot isn't available, suggest the nearest open window."*
  </Card>
  <Card title="Clinic / appointment booking" icon="calendar-check">
    *"Build an agent for a dental clinic. Handle appointment requests (new and existing patients), check-in confirmations, opening-hours questions, and route urgent issues to staff. Don't quote prices."*
  </Card>
  <Card title="SMB FAQ from a website" icon="globe">
    *"Read [your-site.com] and build an agent that answers customer questions about products, pricing, shipping, and returns. Be brief and friendly. If you don't know, say so and offer to take a message."*
  </Card>
  <Card title="Lead capture" icon="user-plus">
    *"Build a lead-capture agent for a B2B SaaS product. Qualify the caller's company size, role, and use case, then book a 15-minute demo on the calendar. Be helpful, not pushy."*
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Tip>
**Bring your own context.** Drop in a URL, paste a document, or attach a PDF in the same Studio Assistant prompt. The richer the context, the better the first scaffold.
</Tip>
