PolyAI builds voice AI agents that handle real customer calls — answering questions, making bookings, resolving issues, and handing off to humans when needed. Powered by [Raven](/agent-settings/raven), a proprietary LLM built for enterprise conversations, PolyAI agents resolve calls naturally without IVR menus or hold queues.

Build, deploy, and monitor agents through Agent Studio's visual interface, the [ADK](/extend/adk) command-line toolkit, or [REST APIs](/api-reference/introduction).
